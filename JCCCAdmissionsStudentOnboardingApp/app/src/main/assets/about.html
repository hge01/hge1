About JCCC

With more than 46,000 students enrolled in credit and noncredit classes each year, Johnson County Community College is one of the state's largest institutions of higher education. Known for excellence in programming and teaching, JCCC offers a full range of undergraduate credit courses and 150 career and certificate programs that prepare students for employment. JCCC's noncredit workforce development program is the largest, most comprehensive in the Kansas City area.

Accreditation
JCCC is accredited by The Higher Learning Commission of the North Central Association of Colleges and Schools
